What is this all about?

  • Connect your datasource (only MySQL for now) and draw charts!

What's the catch?

  • This is a proof of concept application and is more unstable than a drunken monkey on Speed.
  • If your table names or column names contain spaces or funny characters, this will blow up.
  • You can only plot bar charts, that too date and time columns are not supported.
  • It's not been tested. Your database may blow up, your server may melt, your eyes may bleed, or ... you get the point.
  • Nothing is coded defensively. Infact every line in the code has been carefully crafted to offend the delicate sensibilities of every developer who cares about code craftsmanship.
  • If asked, I will publicly disavow having ever written this.

How do I get started?

  • Add a data source (click on the top right).
  • Create a chart. That's it!
